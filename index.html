Hey there!

Earning my Lean Six Sigma Green Belt inspired me to pursue consulting.

I’ve enjoyed bridging business and technical teams, streamlining workflows, and driving customer success—all of which fuel my passion for creating efficient, lean processes.

I’m excited to start a new chapter where I can leverage my adaptable and versatile skill set. Whether it’s embracing change, learning quickly, or simply getting things done, I’m ready to dive in and make a meaningful impact.
